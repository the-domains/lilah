Nuad Bo-rarn

Ancient massage of Thailand

This kind of massage uses no oils or lotions and the receiver remains clothed during a treatment. With a constant body contact between the giver and the receiver, the body is compressed, pulled, stretched and rocked, rather than rubbing on muscles.

For the best practice of the Thai Massage, the receiver will wear loose and comfortable clothing preferably from natural materials, and will lie on a mat or firm mattress on the floor. Also the receiver will be positioned in a variety of yoga-like positions during the massage, combining deep static and rhythmic pressures.

A full Nuad Thai or Thai Massage session may last two hours (120 minutes) and may include pulling fingers, toes, ears, cracking knuckles, walking on the receiver’s back, moving the receiver’s body into many different positions, always with a rhythm which the giver will adjust to fit the receiver.
